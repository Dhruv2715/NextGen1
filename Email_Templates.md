## Quick Tips for Your Email:

1. **Keep it concise** - Busy people appreciate brevity
2. **Lead with value** - Explain what problem your project solves
3. **Highlight key tech** - Mention the impressive technologies you used
4. **Include links** - GitHub repo, live demo (if available)
5. **Call to action** - What do you want them to do? (Review, feedback, demo request)
6. **Professional tone** - Match the formality to your audience
7. **Proofread** - Check for typos and grammar
